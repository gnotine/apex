Valuation Models

The same models used by
professional analysts.

Every model is fully interactive — adjust any assumption and see intrinsic value recalculate instantly.

01
Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)
Projects free cash flow over two phases — high growth then stable — and discounts back to present value using WACC. The gold standard of equity valuation.
V = Σ FCF·(1+g)ⁿ / (1+WACC)ⁿ + TV
02
Dividend Discount Model (DDM)
Two-stage Gordon Growth model for dividend-paying stocks. Projects dividends through an initial growth phase then a perpetuity at the terminal rate.
V = Σ Dⁿ/(1+r)ⁿ + [D·(1+g)/(r−g)]/(1+r)ᴺ
03
Graham Valuation
Three sub-models: the classic Graham Number, the original Graham Formula with growth adjustment, and Net-Net NCAV screening for deep value.
Graham Number = √(22.5 × EPS × BVPS)
04
Reverse DCF
Asks: what FCF growth rate is the current price implying? Solves backwards from market price and shows bear/bull scenarios. Reveals hidden market expectations.
Solve: Price = DCF(g★) → implied growth g★
05
Bond Analytics
Full fixed income toolkit — YTM, current yield, real yield adjusted for inflation, Macaulay duration, and interactive price-vs-yield sensitivity curve.
YTM: solve P = Σ C/(1+y)ᵗ + F/(1+y)ᴺ
06
Real Estate Analysis
Cap rate, cash-on-cash return, GRM, monthly net cash flow, and a 10-year projection of equity build and cumulative cash flow with adjustable appreciation rate.
Cap Rate = NOI / Property Value × 100
